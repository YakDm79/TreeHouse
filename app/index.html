welcome

The company THREEHOUSE has been engaged in turnkey construction of wooden houses for several years. We specialize in wood, as it is the most environmentally friendly, practical and cheapest material today. Log cabins are made of rounded logs at the production site of the enterprise, after which they are delivered to the assembly site and installed on the foundation. Next, the designers get to work.

Our company is ready to provide its clients with all related services, including the development of an individual design project for a future home, bath, restaurant, hotel, hunting lodge, and so on.
